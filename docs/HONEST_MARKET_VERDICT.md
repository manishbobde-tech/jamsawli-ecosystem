# Honest market verdict — would a real entrepreneur ship this? Would temples pay?

**Date:** 2026-07-13  
**Answer in one line:**  
**Almost, but not yet as a feature zoo. Temples pay for money control + festival ops + compliance — not for “AI Hanuman” or gamified bhakti.**

---

## Brutal truth

### What we had (before this pass)
A credible **FaithTech prototype**: multi-tenant skeleton, donate/book, transparency, OTP, tiers, demos, tours.  
Impressive for a hackathon. **Not yet what a 32-trustee board writes a monthly cheque for.**

### Why real temples open the wallet
| They will pay for | They will not pay for (as the hero) |
|-------------------|--------------------------------------|
| 80G receipt that CA accepts | Chatbot personality |
| Cash/hundi + online in one daily total | Devotion leaderboards |
| Festival slot capacity (no double-book chaos) | Voice wake-word demos |
| Weekly “kitna aaya” report for trustees | 14 half-built modules |
| WhatsApp “booking confirmed” | SaaS jargon (MRR, NRR) |
| Counter receipt in <15 seconds | “India’s first AI temple” claims |

### Competitors already own
Compliance ERPs, Kerala pooja sites, POS/kiosks.  
**Our only honest wedge:** modern devotee payments **plus** public trust **plus** counter simplicity **plus** rural-friendly access — sold as **rupee proof**, not as “innovation.”

### Would an entrepreneur keep building this?
**Yes — if the wedge is “Money Desk + Trust Ledger for mid-size temples.”**  
**No — if we keep shipping feature theater without a daily habit for the pujari/clerk.**

### Pricing that matches reality
- Free: online donate + basic counter log (addiction / habit)  
- Growth ₹2,499: daily money desk advanced, weekly report, capacity booking, widgets  
- Trust Pro ₹7,999: 10BD export, full ledger, API, AI assist  

Pay for **hours saved + audit calm + more UPI donations**, not for features.

---

## Product rule going forward

> Every screen must answer: **“Does this help a trustee sleep better about money or a festival queue?”**  
> If no — demote or hide behind Pro.
