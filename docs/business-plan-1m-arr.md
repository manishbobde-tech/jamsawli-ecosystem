# MandirOS - $1M ARR Business Plan

> **Goal:** Achieve $1M (₹8.3Cr) Annual Recurring Revenue within 12-18 months
> **Platform:** Jamsawli Hanuman Lok → Multi-temple SaaS platform (MandirOS)
> **Current State:** Working MVP deployed at jamsawli-ecosystem.vercel.app with 12 temples max ready infrastructure

---

## 1. Business Model (3 Revenue Streams)

### Stream A: Platform Subscriptions (₹3.6Cr/yr = $435K)
- **Free plan** — Basic listing, 1 pooja, standard booking
- **Basic plan @₹999/mo** — Full booking, donations, counter, analytics → **Target: 200 temples**
- **Premium plan @₹4,999/mo** — Premium listing, AI chatbot, WhatsApp, priority support → **Target: 50 temples**

### Stream B: Transaction Fees (₹3Cr/yr = $360K)
- **Donations** — 2% platform fee on all temple donations → Target ₹10Cr/yr processed volume = ₹20L
- **Pooja bookings** — ₹20 flat fee per booking → Target 10K bookings/mo = ₹24L/yr
- **Premium listings** — Featured temple placement, ₹5K-25K/month → Target 30 temples avg ₹10K = ₹36L/yr

### Stream C: Value-Added Services (₹1.7Cr/yr = $205K)
- **Live darshan streaming** — ₹5K setup + ₹2K/mo → 50 temples = ₹12L/yr
- **Prasad delivery logistics** — 5% commission → 5K orders/mo avg ₹500 = ₹15L/yr
- **Digital collectibles** — NFT-like donation receipts, pooja certificates → ₹50K/mo = ₹6L/yr
- **Sponsorship & ads** — Temple events, festival sponsorship → ₹10L/yr
- **Data & insights** — Anonymized devotee analytics for temple management → ₹5L/yr

## 5. Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Jamsawli Anchor (Month 1-2)
- Launch Jamsawli Hanuman Lok as the anchor tenant
- Get 32 trustees to approve and promote
- Piggyback on ₹362Cr govt development for PR
- Collect testimonials, photos, videos for sales deck

### Phase 2: MP Temple Network (Month 3-6)
- Target 50 temples in Madhya Pradesh first (same state)
- Leverage Jamsawli trust network & trustee connections
- Attend temple trust meetings, religious conferences
- Offer 3-month free trial for first 20 temples
- **Sales motion:** WhatsApp + phone calls → demo → onboard

### Phase 3: National Expansion (Month 7-12)
- Target 200-300 temples across India
- Focus on temples with existing digital presence (easier conversion)
- Partner with temple tour operators, Prasad suppliers
- Create referral program: ₹5K bounty per referred temple
- **Sales motion:** Cold email → case study → demo → referral

### Phase 4: Platform Scale (Month 13-18)
- Self-serve onboarding (temples sign up without sales call)
- Viral loop: Devotees ask their local temple to join
- API marketplace for third-party integrations
- Target 400+ temples, $1M ARR
