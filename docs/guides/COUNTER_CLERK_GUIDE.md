# Counter Clerk Guide — MandirOS

**Only what you need at the counter.**  

---

## Login

1. Open `{host}/login`  
2. Prefer **Phone OTP**  
3. Open **Dashboard → Money desk**  

---

## Record a donation (every time cash/UPI at counter)

1. **Amount** (₹)  
2. **Mode:** Cash / UPI counter / Card  
3. Devotee name (optional)  
4. Purpose (general / bhandara / construction)  
5. If they want **80G**: tick box + PAN  
6. Tap **Record & issue receipt**  
7. **Print** if they need paper  

Target: under 10 seconds for simple cash.

---

## Rules

- Log **immediately**, not at night from memory  
- Never invent PAN  
- If payment app fails, still log cash if cash received  
- Don’t change Trust settings or Billing (ask trustee)  
- Don’t share your OTP  

---

## If stuck

Call temple admin / trustee.  
Or MandirOS support with: time + amount + what error showed.
