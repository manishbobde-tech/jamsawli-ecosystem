## Demo script

### 0. Setup (2 min)
- Browser: production  
- Tabs: `/` · `/demo` · `/t/jamsawli-hanuman` · `/dashboard` (staff login)  

### 1. Platform (3 min)
- Open `/` — “This is MandirOS the company product.”  
- Show multi-tenant: every temple gets `/t/slug`.  

### 2. Free vs Pro (8 min)
- `/demo` — toggle Free vs Trust Pro  
- Open `/t/demo-free/pilgrim` → locked features  
- Open `/t/demo-pro/pilgrim` → unlocked  
- “You pay for power when the mandir is ready.”  

### 3. Anchor tenant (8 min)
- `/t/jamsawli-hanuman`  
- Donate form fields (80G)  
- Transparency page  

### 4. Trustee console (10 min) — critical
- Login staff  
- **Money desk** — enter ₹101 cash, show receipt  
- **Weekly report** — share text  
- Billing page — Growth ₹2,499 / Pro ₹7,999  

### 5. Close (5 min)
- CTA: Apply `/admin/temples/new`  
- Next: CS onboarding playbook within 48h  
- Send `/for-trustees` + `/help` links
