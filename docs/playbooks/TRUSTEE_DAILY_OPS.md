# Playbook: Trustee / Temple Daily Ops

**Audience:** Temple trustees & managers (share this PDF/link)  
**Also in-app:** `/help#trustee`  

---

## Every morning (clerk or manager) — 5 minutes

1. Open **Dashboard → Money desk**  
2. Record any overnight cash / counter UPI not yet logged  
3. Glance **today total** (cash + online)  

## During day

- Counter gifts → Money desk immediately (not end-of-day pile)  
- Special pooja walk-ins → Book or counter note  
- SOS / lost-found if Growth+ and pilgrim services on  

## Evening (optional)

- Dashboard → Ops checklist (Growth+)  
- Confirm pending bookings  

## Every Monday (trustee) — 10 minutes

1. **Dashboard → Weekly report**  
2. Read one-liner (kitna aaya)  
3. **Share WhatsApp** to board group  
4. Spot-check 1–2 large donations / receipts  

## Monthly

- Trust settings: 80G numbers still valid?  
- Team page: remove staff who left  
- Billing: plan still matches usage  
- Form 10BD export if Trust Pro + CA needs  

## Escalation

Problem with payments or data → WhatsApp MandirOS CS with slug + screenshot.  
Tax interpretation → temple CA only.
